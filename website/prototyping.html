Prototyping

  • Used 3D printing to create the desired sizes of KetanBot parts
  • Created CAD models of the parts to be printed
  • Iterated on the designs to improve functionality
  • Tested the printed parts for fit and function
  • Made adjustments to the CAD models and reprinted parts as necessary
  • Used the prototypes to inform the final design of KetanBot
